# Roadmap Paragraph

Does the introduction end with a roadmap paragraph? While some view roadmaps as formulaic, they help readers navigate longer papers.

Example: "The remainder of the paper is organized as follows. Section II discusses the history of the introduction and diffusion of television."

Is there a roadmap? If the paper is short enough to not need one, is the structure otherwise clear?
