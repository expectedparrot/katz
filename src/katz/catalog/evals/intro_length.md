# Introduction Length

How long is the introduction? For most empirical economics papers, the recommended maximum is about 3 pages single-spaced (or 5 pages double-spaced). Longer introductions often signal organizational problems — material that belongs in the methods or literature review has crept in.

Estimate the introduction's length and note whether it feels appropriately concise or whether sections could be moved elsewhere.
