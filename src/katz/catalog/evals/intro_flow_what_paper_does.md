# What This Paper Does

Does the introduction clearly state what the paper does and how it works — the methodology in broad strokes? The reader should understand the identification strategy or analytical approach before seeing results.

Example: "In this paper, we identify the effect of preschool exposure to television on adolescent cognitive skills by exploiting variation in the timing of television's introduction to U.S. cities."

Is the approach stated in one or two concrete sentences, or is it vague ("we use data to study...")?
