# Main Findings Stated

Does the introduction state the main findings concretely? The reader should know the paper's answer, not just its question. State the finding, not just the conclusion.

Good: "We find strong evidence against the view that childhood television viewing harms the cognitive or educational development of preschoolers."
Bad: "We find interesting results regarding television and cognitive development."

Are the findings stated with enough specificity that the reader knows what was found, not just that something was found?
