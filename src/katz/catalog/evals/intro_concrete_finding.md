# Concrete Finding Statement

Does the introduction state the main finding concretely — with direction, magnitude, or specific content — rather than just the conclusion?

Good: "We find that the minimum wage strongly reduces work on the intensive margin."
Bad: "The labor supply theory is accepted."
Bad: "We find significant results."

Does the reader know what was found after reading the introduction, or only that something was found?
